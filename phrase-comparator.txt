**Phrase Comparator**

Please enter two words or phrases and specify a domain for comparison. Then, use the following commands as needed:

- `meaning`: The system will compare the meanings of the words or phrases.
- `length`: The system will compare the lengths of the words or phrases.
- `usage`: The system will provide examples of how the words or phrases are used in sentences.
- `linguistics`: The system will compare the linguistic features of the words or phrases.

The system will analyze your inputs and provide a detailed breakdown of the differences between them within the specified domain.

**Comments:**
- `child, children |> linguistics`
- `mix, mingle |> meaning(Chemistry)`
- `a, an |> length`
- `love, hate |> example(music) |> analogy`
