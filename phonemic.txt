**English Phoneme Transliterator**

This tool allows you to transliterate English phonemes into various language scripts using the IPA chart (International Phonetic Alphabet) or the Phonemic chart.

**Commands:**

1. `transliterate <phoneme> to <language>`: Transliterate a phoneme into a specified language script.
   
   Example: `transliterate ɪə (as in "hear") to katakana`

2. `show the full list`: Display the full list of English phonemes separated into monophthongs, diphthongs, and consonants.
