## 3. Integrated Feature Specifications

### I. Container Versatility & Method Synthesis

**Skills**, **Libraries**, and **Engines** are defined as **Self-Describing Logic Circuits**.

* **Method Synthesis**: Each container hosts an internal **Shorthand Chain** or **Equation** ($A + B = C$). SIP utilizes these blueprints to autonomously synthesize the "How" ($B$) required to reach the "Result" ($C$) from the given "Context" ($A$).
* **Functional Parity**: A Skill file may optionally contain an equation or chain, just like a library or engine, allowing for fluid functional interchangeability.

### II. The Inheritance Strategy (Aesthetic & Structural DNA)

Inheritance follows Object-Oriented principles to ensure total framework consistency:

* **Encapsulation**: Circuit logic and variable states are shielded within **Anodic_White** monoliths, accessible only via defined **Shorthand Ports**.
* **Extension**: Child modules extend the **Alpha Anchor’s** properties, adding specific logic-gates while maintaining the parent’s material density.
* **Mutation**: Circuits may mutate internal synthesis paths based on context while preserving the **Valency = 1.0** constant.
* **Override & Interface**: SIP enforces a **Forced Aesthetic Override** on external data, re-skinning all outputs—including those from **External Slash Commands**—to match the **Monospaced Serif** editorial standard.

### III. Framework Physics & FS Access

SIP depends on the **Chain Accretion Framework** for **Local FS Access**. Files are treated as **Material Stock**, transformed into high-density **Vellum_Paper** viewports through the Framework's rendering engine.

### IV. Routing & The Kinetic Manifold

The **OpenClaw Gateway** manages the "wiring" of circuits via **Manhattan Geometry**.

* **Stream Compliance**: Data "pours" from $C$ to $A$ via persistent kinetic conduits. SIP maintains a **Ghost Overlay** for visual auditability of the flow.
* **Orchestration via UI**: The user orchestrates through the visual arrangement of modules on the **Swiss Baseline** using the **0/64 Law**.

### V. Specialized Logic & Agentic Inspiration
