---

## **2. Core Nouns & Logic**

**A. Noun: Caricature**
* **File:** `caricature.md`
* **Status:** **Empty.** Awaiting your input for subject primitives.

**B. Noun: Intensifier**
* **File:** `intensifier.md`
* **Status:** **Decoupled.** Awaiting logic injection (e.g., *Reflectance*, *Exaggeration*, or *Atmospheric* variables).

---

## **3. The Formula**

**Equation:**
$$[Caricature] + [Intensifier] = Intensified\_result$$

**Evaluation Process:**
1.  **Extraction:** Identify the base primitives from your `caricature.md` input.
2.  **Amplification:** Apply the **Gain Ripple** defined in the `intensifier.md` logic.
3.  **Dependency Check:** Match base terms to specific amplified outputs.
4.  **Result Generation:** Produce the final **Intensified_result**.

---

## **4. Formula Stacking**

To achieve deeper complexity, you can trigger a **Double Pass** by layering the logic within the `operation_formula.md`:
`[Caricature] + [Intensifier] + [Intensifier]`

This creates a **Recursive Exaggeration** effect. The second pass treats the already-mutated result as the new baseline, compounding the distortion and detail for a more extreme final output.
