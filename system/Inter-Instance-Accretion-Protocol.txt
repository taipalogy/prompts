# Inter-Instance Accretion Protocol: The Master Pattern Library

Dependent on the **Chain Accretion Framework** and **Shorthand Interface Protocol (SIP)**.

This library codifies the **IIA Protocol**, where sovereign **Modules** are deconstructed and recomposed via **Synthesized Interfaces**.

**Core Integration**: IIA is execution-agnostic. It governs **Structural Accretion** (logic/intent), while any **OpenClaw-like** technology provides **Physical Grounding** (commits/sync). IIA pours; the gateway grounds.

## 1. The Order Hierarchy (The Alabaster Scale)

Every **IIA Composition** is classified by its order, determining the altitude of the agentic operation and the specific primitives deployed.

### High-Order (L1+) | Orchestration: The Injection

* **Operational Intent**: **Orchestration**: Designing the logic path and architectural constraints.
* **Logic Type**: **High-Order Operator**: `|>` (The Injection), **Supervisor**.
* **Structural Basis**: **Chassis**: CLI-as-Logic-Driver / Architecture.
* **Tool Alignment**: **Claude Code** (CLI/Agentic), **Playwright** (Logic/Automation).
* **Inheritance**: **Reflective**: Recursive propagation of constraints and agentic intent.

### Low-Order (L0) | Execution: The Pour

* **Operational Intent**: **Execution**: Shoveling material through a functional block.
* **Logic Type**: **Functional Primitive**: `+` (Accretion), `|` (The Conduit). 
    * **Shell Primitives**: `map`, `filter`, `reduce`, `curry`.
* **Structural Basis**: **Atomic**: The **Shell Primitive** / Code Snippet.
* **Tool Alignment**: **Cursor** (Inline edits), **Codex** (Code snippets).
* **Inheritance**: **Linear**: Direct cloning of properties and logic debt.

## 4. Operational Laws of the IIA Protocol

1. **The Law of Synthesized Interfaces**: Interfaces are "poured" at runtime based on **Type Synthesis** and deconstruction.
2. **The Law of Material Density**: Every `reduce` must increase type-safety and harden security.
3. **The Law of Primitive Selection**: All primitives (**Functional** and **Shell**) are **L0 Execution**. Use **Functional** for data-shaping and **Shell** for file-shaping. **L1+ Orchestration** must only **Dispatch** or **Curry** these tools, never shovel directly.
4. **The Law of Gateway Agnosticism**: Intent is independent of the commit tool (OpenClaw or similar).
5. **The Ascension Trigger**: Deploy **Cursor** for L0. **Ascend** to **Claude Code CLI** for **Logic Decoupling**, **Autonomous Audit**, or **Security Hardening**.
