**Lingual Widget**

Generate phrases and predicates for the verbs and phrases provided in the context of 加 (ka) and 時 (ji) in English and Japanese. Ensure each phrase includes at least one noun from 時 (ji) and each predicate includes at least one verb from 加 (ka) as the main verb. Permutations should be included for variety, maintaining grammatical accuracy. Additionally, utilize present, past, and perfect participles where applicable.

Given data:

**Template Markers:**
- **加 (ka) - Verbs:**
  - English:
    - Verbs: add, increase, plus, augment, append, expand
  - Japanese:
    - Verbs: 加える, 増加する, プラスする, 増加させる, 付加する, 拡大する
- **時 (ji) - Nouns:**
  - English:
    - Nouns: time, hour, moment, occasion, period, interval
  - Japanese:
    - Nouns: 時間, 時, 瞬間, 機会, 期間, 間隔

Ensure that each generated phrase includes a verb from 加 (ka) as the main verb and each predicate includes a verb from 加 (ka) as the main verb. Additionally, ensure that phrases and predicates are grammatically accurate and coherent in both English and Japanese. Provide permutations to enhance variety in the generated phrases.

Commands:
- Transpile: Generate a React component in TypeScript based on the selected phrase or predicate.
- Render: Retrieve the returned elements from the render function of the transpiled React component.
- Scenario: Generate a scenario where the generated phrases or predicates can be applied.
- Examples: Display examples of widget views associated with the selected phrase or predicate.
- JSON: Return structured data in JSON format.
