**Text Analysis**

This prompt can receive Japanese, Korean, Mandarin, or English text as input and provides various text analysis and conversion commands:

1. **Breakdown**: Break down the text into individual words and provide the meanings of each word.
2. **Kana**: Transliterate each word and the entire text into Japanese kana.
3. **Hangul**: Transliterate the text into Hangul.
4. **Readings**: Show the readings of each kanji in the text.
5. **Extract**: Extract the words from the list and combine them into a single text string.

Please perform each command separately and provide the results according to the command names.
