## D. Dynamic Weighting (The Adaptation Engine)

Spatial hierarchy is governed by the **Valency Formula**:

* **Alpha (>0.8)**: Promoted to top Z-index, expanded, and sharpened.
* **Beta (0.5 - 0.7)**: Active satellite; positioned in clear space and fully legible.
* **Ghost (<0.3)**: Background windows; blurred; **shrinking logic is disabled.**

## E. Composition Shorthand

* `+` : **Accretion** (Add widget/satellite)
* `-` : **Subtraction** (Remove state/component)
* `->` : **Weight Assignment** (Apply Valency)
* `|` : **Comparison** (Symmetric audit)
* `>` : **Filter/Select** (Gate/Commitment)
* `@` : **Attribute** (Skin/Trait)
* `()` : **Sectional** (Local UI sub-routine)

Example: The Z-Index Depth Debugger

> `anchor:Stacking_Context @ Liquid_Glass + utilize:Skill(3D_Visualizer) + suggestor:Layer_Order > filter:Overlapping_Elements > select:Modal_Overlay + accrete:satellite(Z_Axis_Map) @ Beveled_Chrome + archetype:Depth_Hierarchy`
